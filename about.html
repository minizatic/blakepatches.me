About

Who I Am

I am a freelance web designer from Manheim, PA. I take pride in making my designs as simple and minimalistic as possible. As Albert Einstein once said,

“Everything should be made as simple as possible, but not simpler.”

I take that philosophy to heart in my designs, adding what must exist, and nothing more. In this busy and cluttered internet of chaos, I strive to seize and perfect the necessary, and cut out all else. What results is a carefully pruned masterpeice, with every piece being part of effort towards an end.
